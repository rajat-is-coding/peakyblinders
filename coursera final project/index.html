All About Peaky Blinders

Introduction

Peaky Blinders is a British historical crime drama television series created by Steven Knight. Set in Birmingham, it follows the exploits of the Peaky Blinders crime gang in the direct aftermath of the First World War. The fictional gang is loosely based on a real urban youth gang active in the city from the 1880s to the 1920s.

Main Characters

  • Tommy Shelby - The ambitious and cunning leader of the Peaky Blinders.
  • Arthur Shelby - Tommy's older brother, known for his volatile nature.
  • Polly Gray - The matriarch of the Shelby family and the gang's treasurer.
  • John Shelby - Another of Tommy's brothers, loyal and fierce.
  • Ada Shelby - The only sister in the Shelby family, who often challenges the family's criminal activities.
